A fun and rewarding experience

Become an iconic part of Australian life and an active member of the Bondi community. Bondi Surf Club is the oldest surf club in the world and our lifesavers are known around the globe! The Club has been a cornerstone in the Bondi community since 1907.

Bronze Medallion Course

The Bronze Medallion course is the core award to become a surf lifesaver in Australia. Bondi Surf Club's courses provide you with the skills and knowledge of patrolling and surf awareness to become a lifesaver.

8-week course including First Aid

Candidates train in groups twice a week for 8 weeks. The course cover both theory and practical skills including CPR, rescues, and patrol operations. Our course also includes a First Aid certificate.

Bronze Medallion courses are open to anyone, aged 15 and over, who can pass the swim test of 400m under 9min. Children ages 13-14 can earn their Surf Rescue Certificate (SRC).

Upon successful completion, lifesavers will participate in regular patrols of Bondi Beach. Patrol requirements are typically 4-5 hours every 3-4 weeks during the summer.
